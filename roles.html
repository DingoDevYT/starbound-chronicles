Crew Roles & Ship Stations

Four classes. One ship. Every crew member controls a station — your class determines your battlefield, your class actions determine how you control it.

4 Core Classes Ship Stations Power Point Economy Alpha v1.0
👨‍✈️
The Captain
Station: The Bridge · Stat Focus: FACE
FACE Primary

This class acts as the general "leader" of a vessel — making strategic decisions for the group and handling comms with other vessels. As they level up, they learn key actions and skills to ensure their vessel runs smoothly in all situations: travel, combat, or otherwise.

LV 10 PP
Tactical Directives
Grant a flat +2 modifier to any single crew member's station dice check during the current Action Phase.
LV 10 PP
Hailing Frequency
Requires a FACE check. This can be used to resolve encounters peacefully or stall an enemy before shots are fired. Effectiveness is at the discretion of the GM to provide you advantage/disadvantage on rolls for good roleplay.
LV 32 PP
Focus Fire
For the remainder of the round, any weapon fired from the ship adds a flat +5 bonus to AD checks.
LV 32 PP
Scramble Signals
Roll a FACE check against the enemy ship. On a success, the enemy vessel is jammed for 1 round, preventing their crew from communicating or using sensors / trackers.
🚀
The Pilot
Station: The Helm · Stat Focus: FLY
FLY Primary

This class acts as the controller of the vessel, deciding the speed and direction of the ship. As a Pilot grows in skill they learn many tricks to get around quicker, dodge attacks, and outmanoeuvre opponents.

LV 11 PP
Full Power!
Increase your baseline tile movement layout for the current round based on the engine component specifications.
LV 12 PP
Evasive Manoeuvre
"Throw the vessel into a violent kinetic drift, spin, or hard roll." Requires a FLY check. On a success, instantly increase your ship's total AD by +10 for the remainder of the current round.
LV 32 PP
Thread the Needle
"Guide the ship flawlessly through dense debris." When utilising map obstacles (like asteroid belts or scrap fields) as cover, the AD penalty applied to enemies trying to target you increases from doubled to tripled for 1 round.
LV 53 PP
Slingshot Drift
"Execute an advanced physics-defying vector turn." Move the ship up to 3 tiles diagonally as a free action, dropping your ship into an opening that completely bypasses the target's obstacle AD protection for your Gunsmith's next attack.
🔫
The Gunsmith
Station: Weapons Array · Stat Focus: FIGHT
FIGHT Primary

The Gunsmith manages the weaponry on board, targeting lock-ons, and ballistic array output as they fight enemies. Built to aim and fire.

LV 1[?]
Fire Weapons
"Unleash a payload from an active, powered hardpoint turret." Requires a FIGHT check rolled directly against the target vessel's Total AD to hit successfully.
LV 11 PP
Target Lock
"Focus the ship's tracking sensors onto a specific hostile hull." Achieving a successful lock grants Advantage on all weapon fire checks against that exact ship on your next turn.
LV 32 PP
Stun Gun
"Fire a stun shot instead of a damaging shot." Requires a FIGHT check rolled directly against the target vessel's Total AD to hit successfully. If completed successfully, the target vessel has their shields "stunned" until the next round.
LV 53 PP
Overcharge Salvo
"Pump raw, unmetered plasma into your active weapon modules." Double the total weapon damage dice pool for this round. However, this creates extreme stress, and makes it harder to aim, reducing your FIGHT check roll by -3.
⚙️
The Technician
Station: Engineering Delta · Stat Focus: FIX
FIX Primary

The Technician regulates the reactor limits, clears heat overloads, and handles emergency repairs. The most strategic of the classes.

LV 10 PP
Reactor Overclock
"Override the safety protocols on the ship's primary power core." Roll a FIX check. On a success, generate +2 bonus PP to be added directly to the crew's power pool on the following round.
LV 11 PP
Shield Restore
"Reroute secondary juice into the deflector grids." Roll a FIX check. Regain 1d20 shield points for the initial cost, plus an additional 1d6 for every extra PP siphoned into the check.
LV 12 PP
Ship Repairs
"Execute a high-stakes mid-combat patch via the ship panels." Roll a FIX check to instantly recover 1d8 lost Hull Points or fix a damaged component's health.
LV 32 PP
Siphon Shields
Siphon hit points from your shield's HP to charge up PP. You can convert as much shield energy as you'd like into PP: 1d10 + 10 damage to shield = 2 PP.
LV 53 PP
System Reboot
Roll a FIX check vs DC 10. On a success, instantly clear all effects (such as stuns etc.) from all components on the ship.
