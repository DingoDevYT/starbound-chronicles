How a Turn Works

Movement: drag your token up to your speed in squares (shown in the bar above). Obstacles block the path.

Core action — pick exactly one:

  • Attack — pick a target and click Attack. The app rolls to-hit (d20 + FIGHT + weapon accuracy) against the target's Total AD (their size + distance + cover), then rolls damage minus their armor automatically.
  • Dash — extra movement instead of attacking.
  • Switch weapon — re-equip a different owned weapon; uses your whole action.

No reactions or bonus actions — move, then do one thing. The GM's manual damage/heal buttons in the Initiative tracker are always available to adjudicate anything unusual.
